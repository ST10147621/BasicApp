## App Installation

### Step 1: Download APK
1. Visit the download link provided for the app's APK file.
2. Download the APK to your mobile device.

### Step 2: Allow Installation from Unknown Sources
1. Go to your device settings.
2. Navigate to **Security** or **Apps settings** (varies by device).
3. Enable **Allow Installation from Unknown Sources**.

### Step 3: Install the APK
1. Locate the downloaded APK file in your **Downloads** folder.
2. Tap on the APK file to start the installation.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

### Step 4: Open the App
1. After installation, tap **Open** to launch the app.
2. Alternatively, find the app icon on your home screen or app drawer.

## User Guide

### Register
1. Tap **Register** in the top navigation bar.
2. Enter the required details (e.g., Name, Email, Password).
3. Tap **Sign Up** to create your account.
4. You can now log in and access the app's features.

### Login
1. Tap **Login** in the top navigation bar.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Tap **Sign In** to proceed.

### Homepage
- The homepage displays featured products and promotions.
- Access navigation options at the top bar for quick links to other sections.

### Shop
1. Tap **Shop** in the top navigation bar.
2. Browse the product catalog.
3. Select a product to view details.
4. Choose a size, and tap **Add to Cart**.

### Cart
1. Tap **Cart** in the top navigation bar.
2. Adjust quantities using the **+** or **-** buttons.
3. Review your items and total cost.
4. Tap **Checkout** to proceed.

### Checkout
1. Enter or confirm billing details (Name, Address, Payment Method).
2. Review your order summary.
3. Tap **Place Order** to complete your purchase.

### Contact Us
1. Tap **Contact Us** in the top navigation bar.
2. Enter your name, email, and message.
3. Tap **Send Message** to contact the support team.

## Admin Guide

### Admin Login
1. Use the same **Login** screen as users.
2. Enter admin credentials (email and password).
3. Tap **Sign In** to access the Admin Dashboard.

### Admin Dashboard
Admins can manage the app via the dashboard.

#### Income Report
1. Tap **Income Report** in the top navigation bar.
2. Use the date picker to select **Start Date** and **End Date**.
3. View income for the selected range.

#### Manage Users
1. Tap **Manage Users** in the top navigation bar.
2. View, edit, or delete user accounts.

#### Add Product
1. Tap **Add Product** in the top navigation bar.
2. Fill in product details (Name, Price, Stock).
3. Upload an image.
4. Tap **Save** to add the product.

#### View Clothes
1. Tap **View Clothes** in the top navigation bar.
2. View a list of all products.
3. Edit or delete products as needed.

#### New Admin
1. Tap **New Admin** in the top navigation bar.
2. Enter the new admin's details (Name, Email, Phone, Password).
3. Tap **Create Admin** to save.

#### Messages
1. Tap **Messages** in the top navigation bar.
2. View messages sent by users.
